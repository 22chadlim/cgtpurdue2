Sebastian Vega

A master of maritime artistry, this artist immerses
themselves in the beauty of the sea. Through
meticulous strokes, they bring seascapes to life,
transcending the ordinary to create a visual odyssey
that invites viewers to contemplate the profound
connection between humanity and the ocean.

Holds a master's degree in Marine Arts from the renowned Royal College
of Art in London, specializing in the nuanced portrayal of seascapes.
Immersed in the awe-inspiring beauty of the ocean, my artistic pursuit
revolves around capturing the profound tranquility and raw power of the sea.
Each brushstroke is a reflection of my deep connection with nature,
and my ambition is to transport  viewers to the serene realms of coastal
landscapes, fostering a sense of  wonder and appreciation for the boundless horizon.
