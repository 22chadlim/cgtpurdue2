About
Daniel Hong

Blending Eastern brushstroke techniques with
contemporary themes, this artist navigates the realms
of tradition and innovation. Their works unfold
narratives that echo the timeless beauty and spiritual
essence of East Asian art, creating compositions that
resonate with cultural depth.

Studied Traditional East Asian Painting at Seoul National University,
delving into the timeless techniques of Eastern artistry.
Rooted in the rich heritage of East Asian art, my work seeks to transcend
temporal boundaries. Through the meticulous practice of traditional techniques,
I aim to infuse my paintings with a contemporary resonance, offering viewers
a visual journey that harmonizes ancient aesthetics with modern sensibilities.
My ultimate goal is to bridge cultures and evoke a deep appreciation for the
beauty embedded in Asian traditions..
